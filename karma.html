Karma and panch tatvas

Published June 2026 • Polygraphs

Karma is performed via panch tatvas. You have your karmic space- if you go to office and do work your space is home to office. If you watch tv all the time, your space is your sofa. Light. This is direction you give to your karma. It follows light. Solidity is any substance added to karma. For example- if you do a job, your substance is your job. Air - this is the substance you consume. In order to thrive and survive, you depend on air. It symbolizes dependency. Liquidity symbolises flow. Autopilot mode of action is a component of liquidity.
